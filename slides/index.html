Vision

Nursing students often enter clinical practice with strong theoretical foundations but minimal hands-on experience navigating digital health systems. When these students transition to clinical settings and encounter an Electronic Medical Record (EMR) software, this inexperience can cause inefficiencies and potential errors. Calvin EMR addresses this challenge by creating a realistic training platform specifically designed for Calvin University's Nursing program students.

Building upon the foundation established by previous students, our team is expanding the system's capabilities by adding features as requested by the Nursing department, while developing a comprehensive collection of end-to-end tests. This enables students to gain practical experience with patient data management and EMR workflows in a completely risk-free environment.

Beyond feature development, we are committed to keeping the codebase clean, testable, scalable, and maintainable. Our ultimate goal is to develop a high-quality educational tool that helps Nursing students learn about digital medical systems — while also establishing a foundation that can be adapted for potential future commercialization.
